Promote your business for free

January 17, 2021 by Amit Kumar

If you have a business in Roorkee that you want to promote online, then look no further.

Roorkee.org allows users to create posts and publish them for free, the post can contain details and one picture about your business
Posts can be created by going to "My Account" Link, logging in with your google account, then clicking the pink "plus" icon and filling in the form.
If you run into challenges feel free to send me an email at admin AT roorkee.org
